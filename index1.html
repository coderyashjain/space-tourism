Lets travel

Space tourism companies offer the opportunity for people to experience the thrill of space travel without having to become astronauts. They typically offer suborbital flights that reach altitudes of up to 100 kilometers, allowing passengers to experience weightlessness and see the curvature of the Earth. Some companies also offer orbital flights that reach altitudes of up to 250 kilometers, allowing passengers to see the ISS and other satellites.
